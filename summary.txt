The act requires the Department of Finance and Administration to develop and maintain a database of all current and future state-funded capital projects. The act requires the database to include, at a minimum, the following information for each project:

1. The name of the project;

2. The total project cost;

3. The source of funding for the project;

4. The project location;

5. The project description;

6. The project status;

7. The date the project is scheduled to be completed; and

8. Any other information the department deems necessary.

The act requires the department to post the database on the department's website and to update the database on a quarterly basis.
